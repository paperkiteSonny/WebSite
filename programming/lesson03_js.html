An Australian hand tugging Portia Woodman’s free-flowing locks may have denied the Black Ferns Sevens a chance to defend their Commonwealth Games gold medal.
The New Zealand flyer looked like she was heading for a match-levelling try in injury time in Sunday’s 17-12 semifinal loss to Australia in Coventry – only to be thwarted by a hair pull.
Woodman, sporting new longer locks for these Games, was charging towards the tryline when Australia’s hat-trick scorer Maddison Levi desperately lunged to make the tackle and yanked a handful of hair.
